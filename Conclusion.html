Conclusion

While it is common for video games to have merchandise like toys and clothing items, Fortnite’s ability to spread itself into such a wide variety of today’s culture is simply uncanny. The game has referenced and even appeared in outside movies, TV shows, video games, and even the real world. The connection it has to both its players and real-world culture is so unique and amazing that it is unlikely another game will accomplish something of this caliber. Fortnite has made itself into such a large cultural phenomenon through its wide appeal and accessibility, integration with other popular media, and iconic dance moves included in the game. Whether the creators of the game had any clue as to how successful the game would be, no one can deny the incredible cultural impact Fortnite has made.

Hunter Ruff-Matthews, Rochester Institute of Technology, 2019
